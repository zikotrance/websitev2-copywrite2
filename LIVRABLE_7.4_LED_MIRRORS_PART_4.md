**Long Description:**

The FLEUVE™ Deep collection creates one of the most captivating visual effects in mirror design: the illusion of endless depth. Using precisely positioned parallel mirrors with integrated LED lighting between them, these installations generate a tunnel of receding reflections that seems to extend into infinity.

The effect is achieved through meticulous engineering—the distance between mirror surfaces, the angle of installation, and the placement of LED strips are all calculated to create the perfect visual experience. The result is a mirror that becomes a conversation piece, a photo opportunity, and a memorable element that guests will remember long after their stay.

Each FLEUVE™ Deep installation is custom-calibrated for its specific location and viewing angles. The LED system can be specified in various colors and programmed for different effects, from subtle ambiance to dramatic pulsing displays. Despite their visual complexity, these installations incorporate the same commercial-grade components and 5-year warranty as all HILO products.
