## Section 3: What's Not Covered

**Titre:**
> Exclusions

**Copy:**

Our warranty is comprehensive, but it doesn't cover everything. The following exclusions apply:

**Not Covered:**

| Exclusion | Reason |
|-----------|--------|
| Intentional damage or vandalism | Beyond normal use conditions |
| Impact damage from objects | Accidental damage, not product failure |
| Abrasive cleaning damage | Improper maintenance |
| Improper installation | Unless HILO partners performed installation |
| Modifications after installation | Changes to original product |
| Acts of nature | Earthquakes, floods, extreme events |
| Normal wear of consumable parts | Expected lifecycle items (batteries, etc.) |

**Important Note:**

These exclusions are narrower than industry standard. Many suppliers exclude humidity damage, normal wear on hardware, and electrical surges. HILO's warranty covers these scenarios because our products are engineered to withstand them.

## Section 4: Claims Process

**Titre:**
> Simple Claims, Fast Resolution

**Copy:**

Filing a warranty claim shouldn't require a lawyer or weeks of waiting. Our process is designed to resolve issues quickly so you can get back to business.

**Step-by-Step Process:**

**Step 1: Report the Issue**

Contact our warranty team via email at warranty@hilo.com, phone at 1-800-HILO-XXX, or through our online claim form. Provide your order number or project name, photos of the issue, and a brief description of the problem.

**Step 2: Claim Review**

Our team reviews your submission within 48 hours. We may request additional photos or information if needed to understand the issue. Most claims are straightforward and require minimal back-and-forth.

**Step 3: Determination**

You receive a claim determination—approved, denied with explanation, or additional information needed. For approved claims, we immediately process replacement orders.

**Step 4: Resolution**

Replacement products ship within 2 weeks for catalogue items, with expedited options available for urgent situations. For installed products, we coordinate removal and reinstallation if HILO partners performed the original installation.

**Average Timeline:**

| Stage | Timeline |
|-------|----------|
| Initial response | Within 24 hours |
| Claim determination | Within 48 hours |
| Replacement shipment | Within 2 weeks |
| Complete resolution | Within 3 weeks |
