## Section 1: Return Policy Overview

**Titre:**
> Returns for Unused Products

**Copy:**

We understand that project needs change. If you have unused catalogue products that you no longer need, here's our return policy:

**Standard Returns:**

| Condition | Policy |
|-----------|--------|
| Timeframe | Within 30 days of delivery |
| Restocking fee | 15% |
| Return shipping | Customer responsibility |
| Product condition | Original packaging, resalable |

**Custom Products:**

Custom products cannot be returned unless they fail to meet approved specifications. This includes:
- Products with custom dimensions
- Products with special configurations
- Products with unique finishes or features

**Non-Returnable Items:**

- Products installed or used
- Products without original packaging
- Products damaged during storage or handling
- Products outside 30-day window

## Section 2: How to Initiate a Return

**Titre:**
> Return Process

**Copy:**

**Step 1: Contact Us**

Email returns@hilo.com or call 1-800-HILO-XXX with:
- Your order number
- Products you wish to return
- Reason for return

**Step 2: Receive Authorization**

We'll review your request and issue a Return Authorization (RA) number within 24-48 hours. Returns shipped without an RA number will not be accepted.

**Step 3: Package Products**

- Use original packaging if available
- Include all components, hardware, and documentation
- Write RA number clearly on the outside of each package

**Step 4: Ship Products**

Ship to the address provided with your RA number. We recommend using a tracked shipping method with insurance.

**Step 5: Receive Refund**

Once we receive and inspect the returned products:
- Refund processed within 5-7 business days
- Refund amount = purchase price minus 15% restocking fee
- Refund issued to original payment method

## Section 3: Warranty Claims

**Titre:**
> Filing a Warranty Claim

**Copy:**

For products that fail during normal use within the 5-year warranty period, follow our simple claims process.

**What's Covered:**

| Component | Coverage |
|-----------|----------|
| Glass & Mirrors | Breakage, delamination, black edges |
| LED Components | Lighting failure, driver malfunction |
| Hardware | Hinge failure, corrosion, breakage |
| Electrical | Connection issues, control malfunction |
| Seals & Gaskets | Deterioration, water infiltration |
| Finishes | Peeling, discoloration, corrosion |

**What's Not Covered:**

- Intentional damage or vandalism
- Impact damage from objects
- Abrasive cleaning damage
- Improper installation (unless HILO installed)
- Modifications after installation
- Acts of nature

## Section 4: Warranty Claim Process

**Titre:**
> Quick Claim Resolution

**Copy:**

**Step 1: Report the Issue**

Contact us via:
- Email: warranty@hilo.com
- Phone: 1-800-HILO-XXX
- Online: [Start a Claim] form

Provide:
- Order number or project name
- Photos of the issue (close-up and context shots)
- Brief description of the problem

**Step 2: Claim Review**

Our team reviews your submission within 48 hours. Most claims are straightforward and require minimal back-and-forth.

**Step 3: Determination**

You receive a claim determination:
- **Approved:** Replacement ships immediately
- **Additional info needed:** We specify what's required
- **Denied:** Explanation provided with options

**Step 4: Resolution**

| Situation | Resolution |
|-----------|------------|
| Catalogue item | Replacement ships within 2 weeks |
| Custom product | Replacement ships within 4 weeks |
| Installed product | We coordinate removal/reinstallation |
| Urgent situation | Expedited processing available |
