### Product Description

**Short Description:**
> PIVOT™ Smart Mirror by HILO® combines full smart functionality with adjustable tilt positioning — ideal for accessible design, senior living, and spaces serving users of varying heights and abilities.

**Long Description:**

The PIVOT™ collection addresses the need for flexible mirror positioning while maintaining full smart mirror capabilities. The precision-engineered pivot mechanism allows 20 degrees of tilt adjustment, ensuring comfortable viewing for wheelchair users, seated guests, and individuals of different heights.

The smart features are designed with accessibility in mind, including enlarged touch targets, voice control options, and compatibility with screen readers. The interface can be simplified for users who prefer basic functions, or expanded for those who want full smart capabilities.

For senior living and accessible hospitality applications, PIVOT™ mirrors include emergency communication features, medication reminders, and integration with care management systems. The tilt mechanism is motorized in upgraded models, allowing users to adjust positioning via voice command or simple button press.
