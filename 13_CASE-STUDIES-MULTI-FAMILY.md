## Project Overview

**Titre:**
> About the Project

**Client Background:**

The client is a regional real estate development company specializing in luxury multi-family residential properties. Their portfolio includes 12 completed developments across major metropolitan areas. The company has built its reputation on premium finishes, extensive amenities, and responsive property management.

The development is a 420-unit luxury apartment complex featuring studio, one-bedroom, and two-bedroom floor plans with high-end kitchen appliances, quartz countertops, and spa-inspired bathrooms designed to differentiate the property in a competitive rental market.

**Project Scope:**

- 280 standard tub/shower enclosures (one-bedroom and two-bedroom units)
- 85 walk-in shower enclosures (premium units)
- 55 corner shower enclosures (studio units)
- All frameless or semi-frameless design

**Key Requirements:**

- Frameless or minimal-frame design for luxury aesthetic
- Durability to avoid 3-5 year replacement cycles
- Hardware quality (corrosion resistance)
- Competitive pricing for project budget
- Coordination with general contractor

---

## The Challenge

**Titre:**
> The Challenge: Previous Shower Glass Investments Were Failing

**Problem Statement:**

The development company had experienced significant issues with shower glass at previous properties. At three earlier developments totaling 850 units, shower glass issues became a significant burden within 5 years:

- Hardware corrosion appearing within 18-24 months
- Glass panels developing stress cracks at attachment points
- Rollers seizing up, making doors difficult to operate
- Seals degrading, allowing water to escape

**Business Impact:**

By year 5 at the earliest property, approximately 15% of original enclosures had been replaced, with projected total replacement cost of over $500,000. Tenant satisfaction scores at properties with aging shower glass lagged behind newer installations.

---

## The HILO Solution

**Titre:**
> Why HILO Was Selected

**Selection Process:**

The development company's procurement team conducted thorough evaluation with specific focus on long-term durability and total cost of ownership. The team visited three completed HILO installations at similar properties.

**HILO Selected Based On:**

- Documented hardware quality (stainless steel and solid brass)
- 5-year warranty covering glass, hardware, seals, and installation
- Engineered designs with proper stress distribution
- Competitive pricing (only 8-10% above budget alternative)
- References from comparable multi-family projects

**Products Selected:**

| Configuration | Design | Hardware |
|---------------|--------|----------|
| Standard tub/shower | Semi-frameless, 3/8" glass | Stainless steel, brushed nickel |
| Walk-in showers | Fully frameless, 3/8" glass | Solid brass, brushed nickel |
| Corner showers | Framed sliding door, 1/4" glass | Stainless steel rollers |
