## SECTION 4 — ELIMINATE GENERIC BEST PRACTICES

### TABLE 10 — Generic Practices to REMOVE

| Generic Practice | Why It's Weak | Replace With | HILO Action |
|------------------|---------------|--------------|-------------|
| **"Contact Us" CTA** | No outcome promise, high friction | "See Your Timeline in 5 Minutes" | IMPLEMENT immediately |
| **"Get a Quote" CTA** | Implies waiting, no instant value | "Get Your Instant Estimate" | IMPLEMENT immediately |
| **Generic Hero Text** | "Welcome to [Company]" or fluffy marketing | Pain-focused: "Your Hotel Opens in 6 Months..." | IMPLEMENT |
| **3-Column Feature Lists** | Dilutes focus, adds cognitive load | Single focused benefit per section | CONSOLIDATE |
| **Long Spec Tables** | Overwhelming, rarely read | 4-5 key differentiators, downloadable catalog | TRUNCATE |
| **Stock Photos** | Undermines authenticity, generic feel | Real project photos OR custom illustrations | SOURCE or REMOVE |
| **"Our Services" Section** | Company-focused, not customer-focused | "How We Solve Your Problem" framework | REFRAME |
| **Multiple CTAs per Page** | Creates decision paralysis | ONE dominant CTA path | CONSOLIDATE |
| **Long FAQ Sections** | Hidden content, low engagement | 3-4 critical questions with schema markup | REDUCE |
| **"Learn More" Buttons** | Passive, no commitment | "See How It Works" or direct to wizard | REPLACE |
| **Company Timeline History** | Self-focused, not relevant to buyer | Focus on client outcomes instead | REPURPOSE |
| **Generic Testimonials** | "Great service!" without specifics | Metric-backed: "Saved 3 weeks on timeline" | REQUIRE metrics |
