## Section 4: Packaging Standards

**Titre:**
> Professional Protection for Fragile Materials

**Mirror Packaging:**

- Corner guards on all vulnerable edges

- Foam edge padding
- Custom crates for large mirrors
- Impact indicators on each package

**Shower Glass Packaging:**

- A-frame crates prevent glass flex during transport
- Foam interlayers between panels
- Edge protectors on all corners
- Hardware packed separately with clear labeling

**Architectural Glass Packaging:**

- Custom A-frames or crates for each order
- Edge protectors and corner guards
- Surface film protection
- Climate-controlled transport available

## Section 7: Delivery Checklist

**Titre:**
> What to Do When Your Order Arrives

**Before Accepting Delivery:**

- [ ] Verify the number of packages matches the packing list
- [ ] Inspect each package for visible damage
- [ ] Check impact indicators for signs of mishandling
- [ ] Note any damage on the delivery receipt before signing

**If Damage Is Visible:**

- [ ] Take photos of damaged packaging
- [ ] Note damage on delivery receipt
- [ ] Contact HILO immediately (before driver leaves if possible)
- [ ] We'll ship replacement within 72 hours

**After Accepting Delivery:**

- [ ] Unpack carefully and inspect all items
- [ ] Verify all items match your order
- [ ] Report any hidden damage within 48 hours
- [ ] Store products in protected area until installation
