### Section 1: Our Story

**Titre:**
> From a Simple Question to 180+ Projects

**Copy:**

HILO began with a question that seemed simple but revealed a deeper problem: why do mirrors and glass products in commercial buildings fail so frequently? The founders, coming from backgrounds in hospitality management and glass manufacturing, had both experienced the frustration of products that looked fine on installation but degraded quickly under real-world conditions.

The hospitality veteran had managed properties where mirror-related maintenance consumed disproportionate staff time and budget. The manufacturing expert had seen how cost-cutting in production created products that met specifications but couldn't withstand daily use. Together, they asked: what would it take to build glass products that actually lasted?

That question led to HILO's founding principle: design and manufacture products that solve real problems for commercial property owners and managers. Not products that meet minimum specifications. Not products optimized for lowest production cost. Products built to perform reliably for years in demanding environments, backed by warranties that reflect genuine confidence in quality.

Fifteen years later, that principle has guided HILO through 180+ projects across North America. From boutique hotels to multi-family developments, from senior living communities to mixed-use urban developments, HILO products have proven that quality construction and honest warranties create lasting value for customers.

### Section 2: Our Approach

**Titre:**
> Different by Design

**Copy:**

HILO's approach differs from typical glass and mirror suppliers in ways that directly benefit commercial customers. We don't compete on lowest price—we compete on best value. This means products engineered for durability, warranties that provide genuine protection, and support that continues long after installation.

**Quality Engineering:**

Every HILO product is designed with commercial use in mind. We specify materials and components that exceed minimum requirements, because minimum requirements are designed for best-case scenarios that rarely exist in real buildings. Our LED components are rated for 50,000+ hours, not the 20,000-hour components found in many "equivalent" products. Our tempered glass meets safety standards with margin to spare. Our hardware is solid stainless steel or brass, never plated finishes that corrode.

This engineering approach costs more upfront but delivers substantially lower total cost of ownership. Customers who track maintenance costs consistently find that HILO products reduce service calls, replacement needs, and associated operational overhead.

**Honest Warranties:**

Our 5-year warranty isn't marketing fluff—it's a genuine commitment to standing behind our products. We've designed products to last well beyond the warranty period, which means claims are rare and our warranty reserve is a genuine safety net rather than a marketing expense. When issues do arise, our claims process is straightforward: describe the problem, provide photos, and we ship replacements. No bureaucracy, no debates about coverage, no pro-rated calculations.

**Direct Relationships:**

HILO sells directly to commercial customers, eliminating distributor markups and ensuring clear communication between manufacturer and end user. This direct model allows us to provide competitive pricing while maintaining the quality standards that differentiate our products. It also means that when customers have questions or issues, they're talking to people who know the products intimately rather than sales representatives for a distribution company.

### Section 1: Products

**Titre:**
> Product Questions

---

**Q: What makes HILO products different from other glass and mirror suppliers?**

HILO products are engineered specifically for commercial applications, not adapted from consumer products. This means industrial-grade components rated for demanding environments, commercial-spec hardware that resists corrosion and wear, tempered safety glass that meets and exceeds code requirements, integrated LED components rated for 50,000+ hours, and comprehensive 5-year warranties that reflect genuine product quality.

We don't compete on lowest price—we compete on best total cost of ownership. Customers who track maintenance costs consistently find that HILO products deliver superior value over their lifespan.

---

**Q: What industries does HILO serve?**

HILO serves commercial customers across hospitality (hotels, resorts, boutique properties), multi-family residential (apartments, condos, student housing), senior living (independent living, assisted living, memory care), commercial office (conference rooms, lobbies, amenity spaces), mixed-use developments, healthcare facilities, and retail and restaurant spaces.

Each industry has unique requirements, and our product lines are designed to address specific use cases within each sector.

---

**Q: Do you offer custom products or only catalogue items?**

We offer both. Our catalogue includes 200+ standard products with instant pricing and 2-3 week delivery. For projects requiring specific dimensions, features, or configurations not available in our catalogue, we offer custom manufacturing with typical 4-6 week lead times.

Custom orders are particularly common for architectural glass applications, historic renovation projects, and projects with unique design requirements. Our engineering team can work with your specifications to develop solutions that meet your exact needs.

---

**Q: What is the difference between your LED mirrors and smart mirrors?**

LED mirrors feature integrated LED lighting with touch dimmer controls, providing high-quality illumination for grooming and general use. They include demister pads to prevent fogging and are available in multiple sizes, color temperatures, and frame styles.

Smart mirrors include all LED mirror features plus an integrated HD display that can show weather, news, calendar information, and other content. Smart mirrors are ideal for premium applications where technology enhances the guest or resident experience. They connect to WiFi and can integrate with building management systems for hotels and senior living communities.

### Section 2: Warranty

**Titre:**
> Warranty Questions

---

**Q: What does the 5-year warranty cover?**

Our 5-year warranty covers all components of HILO products: glass and mirror surfaces (breakage from normal use), LED lighting components and drivers, hardware (hinges, handles, brackets, rollers), electrical components, seals and gaskets, and surface finishes.

The warranty covers both parts and labor for replacement. There's no pro-rating—if a component fails in year 4, you receive a full replacement at no cost.

---

**Q: What is not covered by the warranty?**

The warranty does not cover damage from intentional acts or vandalism, impact damage from objects (beyond normal use), damage from abrasive cleaning products or methods, damage from improper installation (unless HILO partners performed installation), acts of nature (earthquakes, floods, etc.), or modifications made after installation.

For products installed by HILO's certified partners, the warranty also covers installation workmanship.

---

**Q: How do I file a warranty claim?**

Filing a warranty claim is straightforward. First, contact our warranty team at warranty@hilo.com or call 1-800-HILO-XXX. Provide photos and description of the issue, along with your original order information. Our team reviews submissions within 48 hours and provides a claim determination. If approved, replacement products ship within 2 weeks for standard items, or we coordinate repair/replacement for installed products.

There's no complex paperwork, no multiple approval levels, and no arguments about whether something is "really" covered. If it's a product failure under normal use, we make it right.

---

**Q: What is your documented failure rate?**

Across 180+ projects and thousands of installed products, HILO has documented a failure rate of less than 0.5% over the 5-year warranty period. This exceptionally low rate reflects our engineering standards, quality control processes, and commitment to building products that last.

For comparison, industry averages for similar products range from 5-15% failure rates over comparable periods. Our performance data is available for review and has been verified by independent industry analysts.
