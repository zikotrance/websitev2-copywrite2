## Block 2 — Definition Block

LED mirrors are backlit or edge-lit mirror products designed for commercial environments such as hotels, senior living facilities, multi-family residential buildings, and hospitality venues. These mirrors integrate energy-efficient LED lighting behind or around the glass surface to provide even, shadow-free illumination for grooming and vanity applications. Unlike standard mirrors, LED mirrors combine reflective functionality with built-in lighting, anti-fog technology, and touch controls in a single unified fixture. HILO LED mirrors are engineered specifically for high-traffic commercial use, featuring tempered safety glass, 50,000+ hour LED modules, CRI 90+ color rendering, and moisture-resistant construction suitable for bathroom environments across North America.

## Block 14 — FAQ Block

### Frequently Asked Questions About LED Mirrors

#### How long do HILO LED mirrors last?

HILO LED mirrors are rated for 50,000+ hours of LED operation, which translates to approximately seventeen years of use at eight hours per day. The tempered safety glass and moisture-resistant construction are designed to maintain their appearance and function throughout the LED lifespan and beyond. Our 5-year warranty covers all components including the LED modules, glass, electrical connections, and mounting hardware. In practice, most HILO LED mirrors installed in commercial settings continue to perform reliably well beyond the warranty period, with our documented failure rate remaining below 0.5% over five years across 180+ completed projects.

#### Can LED mirrors be installed in wet locations like shower areas?

Standard HILO LED mirrors are rated for damp locations (IP44), making them suitable for bathroom vanity installations where they may be exposed to humidity and occasional water splashes but not direct water contact. For installations directly adjacent to or inside shower enclosures, we recommend consulting with our team about specific moisture protection requirements and potentially customized sealing solutions. Our catalogue specifications include detailed installation guidelines regarding minimum distances from water sources, and our customer support team can provide guidance for any installation scenario that falls outside standard applications.

#### What LED color temperature should I choose for my project?

The ideal LED color temperature depends on your design intent and the environment where the mirrors will be installed. Warm 3000K creates a welcoming, relaxed atmosphere that works well in boutique hotels and residential-style settings. Neutral 4000K provides a clean, professional appearance suitable for most commercial applications including offices and standard hotel rooms. Cool 5000K delivers the brightest, most clinical illumination, making it ideal for healthcare facilities, fitness centers, and senior living communities where maximum visibility is a priority. All HILO LED mirrors feature CRI 90+ color rendering regardless of color temperature, ensuring accurate color representation for grooming and makeup application.

#### Do HILO LED mirrors require special electrical wiring?

HILO LED mirrors are designed for straightforward electrical integration into standard commercial construction. Most models are available in both hardwired and plug-in configurations, allowing flexibility depending on your project's electrical plan. Hardwired models connect to a standard 120V junction box behind the mirror, which is typically specified during the rough-in electrical phase. Plug-in models include a discreet power cord that can be routed to a standard outlet. All HILO LED mirrors should be installed by a licensed electrician in accordance with local electrical codes. Our installation partners are experienced with the electrical requirements and can handle the complete installation process if needed.

#### What happens if an LED mirror arrives damaged?

HILO's damage-free guarantee means that if any mirror arrives damaged, we ship a replacement at no additional cost and with no claims process required. Simply document the damage with photos upon delivery and contact our support team. We immediately initiate a replacement shipment—typically within 48 hours for catalogue items—so your installation schedule is minimally impacted. This guarantee applies to all orders shipped through our door-to-door delivery system and covers damage from any cause during transit. We use purpose-built packaging with corner protection, shock-absorbing materials, and impact indicators to minimize the risk of damage in the first place.
